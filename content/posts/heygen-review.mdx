Avatar IV videos consume **20 credits per minute** from the premium credit pool. On the Creator plan, that means 200 credits = 10 minutes of Avatar IV video per month. This is important to understand before selecting a plan.

### Video Translation with Lip Sync

HeyGen's **Video Translation** feature takes an existing video — filmed or avatar-generated — and produces a localized version in any of 175+ supported languages. The process:

1. Transcribes the source audio
2. Translates the script to the target language
3. Generates new audio in the target language using AI voice synthesis or voice cloning
4. Resynchronizes the presenter's lip movements to the new audio

The result is a localized video where the presenter appears to be speaking the target language natively. For organizations producing marketing content for global markets, the economics are compelling: produce once in English, localize to 20 languages without per-language production overhead.

Video translation consumes **5–10 credits per minute** depending on features enabled — significantly more credit-efficient than Avatar IV.

### Interactive Avatar

**Interactive Avatar** is HeyGen's real-time conversational AI product. Rather than a pre-rendered video, Interactive Avatar is a live AI avatar that responds to user input in real time — effectively an AI chatbot with a photorealistic human face delivering the responses.

Use cases include:

Interactive Avatar consumes **20 credits per minute** of live interaction — the same rate as Avatar IV video. This is a significant credit cost for extended conversations.

### Voice Cloning

Voice cloning is available on Creator and above. Combined with the Custom Digital Twin avatar (available as an add-on), this enables a fully digital version of a specific individual that can produce video content indefinitely.

## The Credit System Demystified

The credit system is HeyGen's most frequently misunderstood element, and the source of most buyer dissatisfaction. Here is the honest breakdown.

**Standard avatar videos do not consume premium credits.** If you use a standard AI avatar (not Avatar IV) on the Creator plan, unlimited videos up to 30 minutes each are included with no credit cost. This is genuinely unlimited — not a soft cap.

**Premium credits are consumed only by advanced features:**

| Feature | Credit Cost |
|---------|-------------|
| Avatar IV video | 20 credits/minute |
| Video translation with lip sync | 5–10 credits/minute |
| AI image generation | 2 credits/image |
| AI video asset generation | 15 credits/video |
| Interactive Avatar (live) | 20 credits/minute |

**What Creator's 200 monthly credits actually buy you:**

- Avatar IV video: 200 ÷ 20 = **10 minutes/month**
- Video translation: 200 ÷ 7.5 (average) = **~27 minutes/month**
- Interactive Avatar: 200 ÷ 20 = **10 minutes/month**
- AI image generation: 200 ÷ 2 = **100 images/month**

This means a Creator subscriber who primarily wants Avatar IV realism gets **10 minutes of Avatar IV video per month** before exhausting premium credits. That is enough for roughly 3–5 short marketing videos — which is usable but more limited than the unlimited headline implies.

**Credits do not roll over.** Unused credits at the end of the billing cycle are lost. If you produce nothing in a given month, you lose 200 credits.

**Extra credits** are available as add-ons: $15/month for 300 credits, or $150/year for 3,600 credits. This is a reasonable option for months with higher production volume.

**The API is completely separate** from all web plans. If you need to call HeyGen programmatically — for automated pipelines, CRM integrations, or bulk video production — you pay separately at **$1/minute of video generated**, regardless of what web plan you subscribe to.
