## What Is Adobe Firefly?

Adobe Firefly is Adobe's AI generation platform, launched in 2023 and substantially expanded through 2025–2026. It operates both as a **standalone web app** at firefly.adobe.com and as an integrated feature inside Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and Adobe Express.

What sets Firefly apart from every other AI generation tool is its training data policy: Adobe trained Firefly exclusively on **Adobe Stock assets (licensed), openly licensed content, and public domain materials**. It explicitly did not train on scrapped images from the open web — a deliberate decision that addresses the legal uncertainty surrounding other AI image tools.

For enterprise customers, Adobe goes further with **IP indemnification**: Adobe legally defends customers against copyright infringement claims related to Firefly outputs. This is rare in the AI space and meaningful for agencies and brands with legal exposure.

By May 2026, Firefly has expanded well beyond image generation. It now covers:
- Text to image and text effects
- Generative Fill and Generative Expand for photo editing
- AI video generation (text-to-video and image-to-video)
- Audio translation with lip sync
- Access to partner AI models (Google Veo 3, Runway, ChatGPT Image, and others)
- Integrations with the full Adobe Creative Cloud suite

## The Credit System Explained: Standard vs Premium

Before evaluating Firefly's features, you need to understand its credit system — because it directly affects the real cost of using the platform.

Firefly splits features into two tiers:

### Standard Features — Unlimited on All Paid Plans

Standard features consume **no credits** on any paid plan. You can use them as much as you want. These include:
- Basic text-to-image generation
- Generative Fill (in Photoshop)
- Generative Expand
- Vector recolor (in Illustrator)
- Text effects
- Basic background removal

For most day-to-day design work, standard features cover the majority of use cases — and they're unlimited. This is genuinely good value.

### Premium Features — Consume Credits

Premium features draw from your monthly credit allocation. Key examples and costs:
- **AI video generation:** 100 credits per 5-second clip
- **Video translation with lip sync:** 5 credits per second of video
- **Partner model generations** (Veo 3, Runway, etc.): variable credits
- **Fast mode:** 2x the credit cost of standard mode for faster generation

The credit math matters: if you're on the Standard plan (2,000 credits/month) and you want to generate videos, that's 20 five-second clips per month. On the Pro plan (4,000 credits), that's 40 clips. On Firefly Premium (50,000 credits), video generation becomes viable at studio scale.

**Important:** Credits reset monthly and **do not roll over**. Unused credits expire. Plan your production schedule accordingly.

## Key Features

### Generative Fill and Generative Expand

These are Firefly's standout features and the reason professional designers keep coming back. Both are integrated directly into Photoshop.

**Generative Fill** lets you select any area of an image and describe what you want there. Remove a distracting element, add a new background object, replace a product with a different color variant, or fill in gaps in a composition — all with a text description. The results are photorealistic, edge-aware, and match the lighting and perspective of the surrounding image.

**Generative Expand** extends images beyond their original borders. Upload a portrait in 1:1 format and expand it to 16:9 for a banner — Firefly fills in the sides coherently with context-aware generation. This alone saves hours of manual compositing work per week for designers handling multiple format deliverables.

Both features use standard credits on paid plans (unlimited usage), making them cost-free for everyday professional work.

### AI Video Generation

Firefly's AI video generation converts text prompts or existing images into short video clips. The quality uses Google's Veo 3 model (available via the partner models integration), which produces noticeably cinematic motion and consistent physics compared to earlier AI video tools.

**Cost reality check:** At 100 credits per 5-second clip, video generation is the most expensive use of your Firefly credit allocation. On the Standard plan (2,000 credits), you get 20 five-second clips — roughly 100 seconds of AI video per month. For studios and agencies with dedicated video budgets, the Firefly Premium plan (50,000 credits, $199.99/month) makes more sense. For individual creators, this may be a limiting factor.

### Partner Models

One of Firefly's most underrated 2026 features is access to **partner AI models from within the Firefly interface**. Instead of juggling separate subscriptions and interfaces, Firefly subscribers can access:

- **Google Veo 3** — video generation
- **Runway** — video generation and editing
- **ChatGPT Image** (DALL-E) — image generation
- **Black Forest Labs** — image generation (Flux models)
- **Ideogram** — text-in-image specialist
- **Luma AI** — 3D and video
- **ElevenLabs** — voice and audio

This consolidation is genuinely valuable: instead of paying for five separate AI tool subscriptions and managing five different interfaces, Firefly becomes a single creative hub with access to the best models across categories. Credits used on partner models vary by model.

### Text to Image and Text Effects

Firefly's core text-to-image generation is solid for commercial work — clean, professional, and reliably usable as stock photography replacements. For marketing materials, product imagery, backgrounds, and illustrations, the quality is consistently good.

**Text Effects** applies AI-generated styles to typography — wrapping words in specific textures, materials, or environments. Useful for social media graphics, thumbnails, and display advertising that needs visually distinctive headline treatment.

### Audio Translation with Lip Sync

Firefly's audio translation feature converts video narration to other languages while **matching the speaker's lip movements** to the translated audio. This is the same technology being commercialized by HeyGen, but integrated into Adobe's platform for Creative Cloud subscribers.

For global marketing teams producing video content that needs to run in multiple markets, this feature alone can eliminate significant translation and production costs.

### Content Credentials

Every Firefly-generated asset can be tagged with **Content Credentials** — embedded metadata indicating the content was AI-generated, which model was used, and when it was created. This is part of Adobe's participation in the Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity (C2PA) standard.

For brands concerned about AI disclosure requirements (which are becoming increasingly regulated globally), Content Credentials provide a defensible audit trail.
