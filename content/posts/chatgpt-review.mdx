## Key Features

### GPT-5.4: One Model, Three Superpowers

Previous ChatGPT versions required users to toggle between models — GPT-4o for general chat, o1 for reasoning, Codex for code. GPT-5.4 eliminates this friction by combining all three into a single model that adapts its behavior to the task.

In practice: you ask it to debug a Python script, and it reasons through the problem, writes corrected code, and explains what went wrong — all without you manually switching modes. For everyday users, this is a significant quality-of-life improvement.

### DALL-E Images 2.0 — Native Image Generation

ChatGPT's native image generation received a major upgrade with **Images 2.0, launched April 21, 2026**. The new system produces noticeably sharper, more photorealistic outputs with better instruction-following — particularly for text rendering inside images, which was previously a known weakness.

Image generation is available on **Plus and above**. On the Plus plan ($20/month), you get a generous monthly allocation of image generations — enough for most creators and marketers. Images can be generated in multiple aspect ratios, edited in-canvas, and exported at full resolution.

This remains one of ChatGPT's clearest advantages over Claude, which **has no image generation** at all.

### Sora — Video Generation

**Sora** is OpenAI's AI video generation model, available to Plus subscribers and above. Sora can generate short video clips from text prompts, transform existing images into video, and apply stylistic effects to footage.

As of May 2026, Sora is not yet a replacement for professional video tools — clips are still limited in length and occasional artifacts appear in complex motion sequences. But for social media content, concept visualization, and short-form marketing clips, it's genuinely useful and improves with every model update.

No other AI assistant in the mainstream tier offers integrated video generation at the Plus price point.

### Agent Mode — Autonomous Web Tasks

**Agent Mode** is ChatGPT's answer to the demand for AI that does things, not just says things. With Agent Mode enabled, ChatGPT can:

- Navigate websites and extract information
- Fill out forms and complete multi-step web workflows
- Book appointments and manage calendars
- Research topics across multiple sources and synthesize findings

Agent Mode is available on Plus and above. While early versions were limited and occasionally unreliable, the GPT-5.4 upgrade has significantly improved accuracy and task completion rates. For repetitive research tasks — comparing prices, gathering competitor data, summarizing news — Agent Mode is a genuine time saver.

### Deep Research — Citation-Backed Reports

**Deep Research** generates long-form, thoroughly sourced reports on any topic. Unlike a standard web search response, Deep Research spends several minutes actively browsing sources, evaluating credibility, and synthesizing findings into a structured document with inline citations.

- **Plus plan:** 10 Deep Research runs per month
- **Pro $200 plan:** 250 Deep Research runs per month

For professionals who regularly produce research-backed documents — analysts, journalists, consultants, academics — 10 runs per month is often enough on the Plus plan. Power users needing daily research reports will want to consider Pro.

### Advanced Voice Mode

ChatGPT's **Advanced Voice Mode** supports natural, real-time spoken conversation with low latency. You can interrupt mid-sentence, the model adapts tone and pacing to context, and the voice quality is significantly better than traditional text-to-speech systems.

Voice Mode is available on mobile (iOS and Android) and increasingly on desktop. It's particularly useful for hands-free research during commutes, verbal brainstorming sessions, and accessibility use cases.

### Custom GPTs Marketplace

The **Custom GPTs** ecosystem is one of ChatGPT's most underrated advantages. OpenAI's GPT Store hosts thousands of specialized AI tools built by third-party developers — custom assistants for legal drafting, language tutoring, code review, SEO, and virtually every professional niche.

While the quality varies widely, the best Custom GPTs provide genuinely specialized capabilities that a general-purpose assistant can't match out of the box.
